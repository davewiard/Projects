At ZOOM+Care, our mission is to deliver radical access to Quality Care. Not just Urgent Care — all your care.

Schedule same-day, no-wait visits for Urgent Care, Primary Care, Specialist Care, and Emergency Care at 30+ clinics in Portland, Seattle, Salem, and Vancouver. We’re open 365 days a year, early morning and late nights, with meds and labs on site. 


Zoom’s medical teams are lead by board-certified MDs including family practitioners, gynecologists, dermatologists, psychiatrists, and ER physicians, who work with our naturopaths, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants to ensure a consistent standard of care based on the latest science.


We’ve proven that Team Care is Safer Care. Faster Care is Better Care. And we’re right in the neighborhoods where you work, play, and shop.

Get all your care. On-demand.
